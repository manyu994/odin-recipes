Poha

Description

Poha is a light and flavorful Indian breakfast dish made with flattened rice, spices, and peanuts. Originating from Maharashtra and popular across India, this dish is quick to make and perfect for a wholesome start to the day. Tempered with mustard seeds, curry leaves, turmeric, and green chilies, Poha has a mild yet delicious taste. Garnished with fresh coriander and a squeeze of lemon, it pairs well with a cup of hot chai!

Steps

Preparing the Poha

  1. Place poha in a strainer and rinse under running water for 5-10 seconds. Drain and set aside.
  2. Sprinkle salt and sugar over the poha and mix gently.

  2. Add peanuts and fry until crispy. Remove and set aside.
